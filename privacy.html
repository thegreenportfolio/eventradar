Event Radar – Privacy & Safety Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Event Radar (“the App”) helps users search for public events by country, city, category, and date range. The App is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. No account or sign-in is required to use the App.

Event Radar allows users to search for events, view event details, save selected events locally on their device, open event websites when available, and optionally add saved events to Apple Calendar.

2. No Account Required

Event Radar does not require users to create an account. The App does not collect login credentials, usernames, passwords, or profile information.

3. Local Saved Events

When you save events in Event Radar, those saved events are stored locally on your device.

Saved event information may include details such as event title, date, venue, city, country, category, event website link, and other event details returned by public event sources.

Saved events are used only to display your saved event list inside the App and to help you add selected events to your calendar if you choose to do so.

4. Event Search

Event Radar searches for public event information using event data providers and publicly available event sources. Search requests may include the country, city, category, and date range selected by the user.

The App does not use your personal identity to perform event searches. Search results depend on the availability and accuracy of third-party event sources.

5. Calendar Access

Event Radar may request access to Apple Calendar so you can add selected saved events to your calendar.

Calendar access is used only when you choose to add an event to your calendar. Event Radar does not use calendar information for advertising, tracking, or profiling purposes.

You can manage calendar permissions at any time in your device Settings.

6. Website Links

Event Radar may provide links to external event websites when available. Opening an external website may take you outside the App or open a web page provided by a third party.

External websites are not controlled by Event Radar. Their own privacy policies, terms, ticket prices, availability, and event details may apply.

7. Third-Party Services and Event Sources

Event Radar may use third-party event data providers or public event sources to display event information. These sources may process search-related information such as selected city, country, category, and date range in order to return event results.

Event Radar does not sell your personal data and does not use advertising trackers for user profiling.

8. Data Sharing

Event Radar does not sell your personal data.

The App does not share your saved local events with advertisers. Saved events remain on your device unless you choose to add them to Apple Calendar or open an external event website.

9. Data Deletion

You can remove saved events from within the App where available. You can also delete local app data by removing the App from your device.

Because Event Radar does not require an account, there is no online account to delete.

10. Event Accuracy and Availability

Event Radar displays event information from third-party sources and public event listings. Event details such as date, time, venue, availability, price, cancellation status, and ticket information may change.

Before attending or purchasing tickets, users should confirm event details directly with the official event organizer, venue, or ticket provider.

11. Safety Guidelines

Event Radar helps users discover events, but users are responsible for making safe decisions when attending any event.

  • Confirm the event details before going.
  • Use official ticket providers and trusted websites.
  • Be careful when visiting unfamiliar locations.
  • Follow local laws, venue rules, and safety instructions.
  • Do not share personal information with unknown third parties.

12. Children’s Privacy

Event Radar does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise use of the App by minors, especially when opening external event websites or attending events.

13. Changes

This Privacy & Safety Policy may be updated in the future if features change. Any updates will be published at the same URL.
